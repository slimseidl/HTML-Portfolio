Professional Life

This entails the development and continuous improvement of processes throughout the plant including ERP software functionality, manufacturing processes, and best practices. A good portion of my job also includes data mining and analytics. I have a passion for utilizing data to drive strategic business decisions.

Before taking on a role as a Process Manager, I was a continuous improvement engineer for the same company. As the company grew, the need for a larger role in development and analytics materialized so I jumped at the opportunity. This new role opened a door to the world of analytics through relational databases, SQL, business intelligence tools and more. I started out knowing very little about analytics but taught myself through videos and online forums. The hunger for knowledge constantly increased and still does on a daily basis which opened up the possibility of seeking out professional training.

Fall 2023 was when I decided to go back to school. I enrolled at Western Governor's University in the Data Analytics B.S. degree program. I am currently taking courses through WGU with an estimated finish date of summer 2025. As part of the degree program, I've learned languages such as SQL, Python, and R, to name a few. I also dipped my toes into the waters of web development, which I thoroughly enjoyed. Additional resources were acquired through my enrollment at WGU, which includes a subscription to Udemy.

Through Udemy, I've had the chance to enroll in a coding bootcamp that's allowed me to obtain more skills relating to the web and software development world. I plan to finish out my Data Analytics B.S. while working on personal web development projects and building my portfolio. Stay tuned here for more.
