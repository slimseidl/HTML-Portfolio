The Best Movies According to John

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Twister

This is a classic. The weather phenomenon of tornadoes hits close to home for a guy from the Midwest.

Step Brothers

This is a bold take. Many people may disagree with the raunchy humor, but I could watch this movie over and over again.

War Dogs

I'm a sucker for films based on true stories. This film featuring Miles Teller & Jonah Hill keeps you on the edge of your seat.
